Basics

  • We will randomly draw 3 winners
  • Each winner chooses 1 prize from the pool
  • There are 10 different ways to enter
  • Enter through all 10 ways to increase your odds
  • Entry methods that are worth more than 1 "point" put your name in the virtual hat multiple times
  • The contest will run until Sunday, October 16 at 11:59 PM EST

How to Enter

  • [2x] Email us (podigious@gmail.com) with the secret code word from our Tri Chapter 3 episode
  • [2x] Tumblr: reblog the announcement post
  • [1x] Tumblr: like the announcement post
  • [2x] Twitter: retweet the announcement tweet
  • [1x] Twitter: like the announcement tweet
  • [2x] Facebook: comment on the announcement post
  • [1x] Facebook: like the announcement post
  • [2x] Reddit: comment in the giveaway thread
  • [5x] Support us on Patreon (we'll automatically enter every current patron into the giveaway)
  • [10x] Rate the podcast on iTunes and email us (podigious@gmail.com) a screenshot of your review (if you've ever done this, you're automatically entered for this and any future giveaway)

Entry Requirements

  • The giveaway is open worldwide, and we'll ship internationally.
  • You must be comfortable with giving us your name and mailing address if you win so that we can send you your choice of prize.
  • You must be responsive on the social media platforms you use to enter the giveaway. For example, if you've entered on both Facebook and Tumblr, make sure you check your inboxes on both of them when the contest ends. On Twitter, we'll reach out to the winner with a DM, so you must have DMs open or follow us. On Tumblr, we'll reach out with a message from Jeff's primary (but mostly unused) Tumblr blog, so you need to have messaging open from everyone. If we try to reach out and can't get in touch, then we'll draw a different winner.
  • We'll announce and reach out to the winners on October 16. If we don't hear back from a winner by 11:59 PM EST on October 17, then we'll draw a new winner to replace them.

Clarifications

  • Leaving an iTunes review and emailing us a screenshot enters you into this and all future giveaways. So, if you've already left a review and sent us a screenshot, you don't need to do it again (unless you need to be found at a new email address).
  • We've tried to weigh the entries rewarded for each entry method so that methods which require more effort give more reward.
  • If you enter with all 10 methods, you will have a total of 28 entries.
  • We haven't pre-calculated shipping expenses for all items to all countries, so there's a chance that shipping a large/heavy item to your country might cost a ludicrous amount, in which case we may ask you to pick a smaller prize. Everything we've shipped before has been fine, so hopefully this doesn't come up.
  • Let us know if you have any questions!
