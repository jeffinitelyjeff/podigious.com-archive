Hey Digi-fans! To celebrate the release of Tri Chapter 2, we're holding another giveaway of the Tri prizes Jeff and Ashley got from their recent trip to Japan (plus some cool new stuff Jeff's imported since the last giveaway!). There'll be a total of 3 winners, and each winner will get to choose 1 prize from the pool. There are 11 different ways to enter, and you can enter multiple ways to increase your odds. The contest will run until March 21 at 11:59 PM EST.

We also discussed the giveaway rules in our Tri Chapter 2 flashcast.

May the odds be ever in your digi-favor!

How to Enter

  • Tumblr: reblog the announcement post
  • Tumblr: like the announcement post
  • Twitter: retweet the announcement tweet
  • Twitter: like the announcement tweet
  • Facebook: comment on the announcement post
  • Facebook: like the announcement post
  • Reddit: comment in the giveaway thread
  • Email us (podigious@gmail.com) with the secret code word from episode F02
  • Email us (podigious@gmail.com) with the secret code word from the upcoming episode T2.0
  • [5x] Support us on Patreon
  • [10x] Rate the podcast on iTunes and email us (podigious@gmail.com) a screenshot of your review

Entry Requirements

  • The contest is open worldwide.
  • You must be comfortable with giving us your name and mailing address if you win so that we can send you your choice of prize.
  • You must be responsive on the social media platforms you use to enter the contest. For example, if you've entered on both Facebook and Tumblr, make sure you check your inboxes on both of them when the contest ends.
  • You must be reachable on the social media platform you use to enter the contest. On Twitter, we'll reach out to the winner with a DM, so you must have DMs open or follow us. On Tumblr, we'll reach out with a message from Jeff's primary (but mostly unused) Tumblr blog, so you need to have messaging open from everyone. If we try to reach out and can't get in touch, then we'll draw a different winner.
  • We'll announce and reach out to the winners around March 22 at 12:01 AM EST. If we don't hear back from a winner by 11:59 PM EST on March 22, then we'll draw a new winner to replace them.

Clarifications

  • Leaving an iTunes review and emailing us a screenshot enters you into this and all future giveaways. So, if you've already left a review and sent us a screenshot, you don't need to do it again (unless you need to be found at a new email address).
  • Being a Patreon supporter or leaving an iTunes review give you more entries because they both require more effort than the other methods.
  • If you enter with all 11 methods, you will have a total of 24 entries.
  • Let us know if you have any questions!
