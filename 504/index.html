504. The Fridging of Frigimon

Download (97.3 MB) • YouTube • SoundCloud • • Feb 12, 2018

It's fightin' time again! Yoshi babysits Marcus and Keenan, Marcus fights a house, Gotsumon gets an army, SaberLeomon learns the meaning of his name, Gotsumon becomes Gonetsumon, Kurata brags about starting the Crier Forest Fire, and Kurata eats crow.

In this episode, we continue to debate whether Marcus is a Good Boy, Ashley cries over Merukimon, Jeff looks at the contrasting "villainy" of Merukimon and Kurata, we discuss the real-world politics Data Squad reflects, and we discover Kurata's one true gender.

Thanks for listening!
