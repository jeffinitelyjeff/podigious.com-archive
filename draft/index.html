Tamers Fantasy Draft

Welcome to the Tamers Fantasy Draft! It's the Podigious draft which is actually kind of like a normal sports draft (here's looking at you, Ridiculous Dialogue Draft).

Basics

  • In each main Tamers episode (the ones numbered 3XX), each host will pick their favorite character for that arc.
  • Before the episode comes out, you draft a team of 1 human and 1 Digimon character (this team lasts for just the upcoming podcast episode)
  • You get a point each time a character from your team is chosen as one of our favorites (in that podcast only)
  • In the feedback episode, we'll also award a bonus point to the draft team which we find most ridiculous and a bonus point to the draft team which we most wished were actually canon (if we can't reach a consensus on the one point to award for either catagory, we'll give out multiple points)
  • At the end of the season, we'll send a prize to the listener with the most points: a copy of the English Tamers DVD Boxset and a Taichi & Agumon G.E.M figure. We'll ship internationally.
  • Leaderboard: coming soon

How to Enter

  • You can enter on our forum or via social media on Twitter, Tumblr, or Facebook.
  • In the week before an episode comes out, we'll post calling for draft picks. Reply to one of these posts with your picks to enter!
  • The draft will generally open around the time the previous episode's feedback goes up (about a week before), but the schedule can vary a lot. If you follow us on social media, we'll keep you informed of the upcoming schedule.
  • We advise that you pick one platfrom to enter and use that same platform each episode to make sure that all of your points get added to the same listener in our leaderboard.

Frequently Asked Questions

If you have any questions, feel free to ask us on social media. We'll update this space with any common questions. Have fun, and good luck!
