Entry Requirements

  • You must be comfortable with giving us your name and mailing address if you win so that we can send you your choice of prize.

Clarifications

  • Leaving an iTunes review and emailing us a screenshot enters you into this and all future giveaways. So, if you've already left a review and sent us a screenshot, you don't need to do it again (unless you need to be found at a new email address).

  • If you enter with all 9 methods, you will have a total of 26 entries.
  • We haven't pre-calculated shipping expenses for all items to all countries, so there's a chance that shipping a large/heavy item to your country might cost a ludicrous amount, in which case we may ask you to pick a smaller prize. Everything we've shipped so far has been fine, so hopefully this doesn't come up.
